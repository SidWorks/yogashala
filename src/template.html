About me

Designed by Sadhguru, Yoga Sutra: Classical Hatha Yoga offers an unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years.

Aman Agrawal undertook the internationally acclaimed 1750-hour Isha Hatha Training Programme in South India, wishing to bring Hatha Yoga in its full depth and profundity. He is one of the founder members of Yoga SUTRA, dedicated to profound science for the wellbeing of everyone.Yoga SUTRA has been established with Sadhguru’s vision of making “yoga” a lifestyle choice for each one of us. He has lived, studied and worked across differnt parts of India-Nagpur, Pune, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Uttar Pradesh & Bihar. Also a graduate in Electronics Engineering, life has offered him a range of conventionally rewarding career. He is equipped to teach over 48 unique modules : 1 day, 3 day, 5 day, 1 week, 2 week, 1 month and ongoing sessions.

Bhuta Shuddhi

Bhuta Shuddhi means “purification of the elements”. It is the basis of all yogic practices. The basis for all creation, including the physical body, is the group of five elements: earth, water, wind, fire, and space. The wellbeing of the body and mind can be established by purifying these five elements within the human system. This process also shapes the body into a stepping stone towards one’s ultimate wellbeing. There is a whole system of yoga called Bhuta Shuddhi, which means “purification of the elements.” The Bhuta Shuddhi processes offered by Isha provide a unique opportunity for everyone to benefit from this esoteric yogic science, which otherwise requires intense sadhana (spiritual practices).
